AFRICAN FICTION FESTIVAL 2023

A yearly festival to remind us all of the roots and purpose of African literature, creating a positive change with people from all 55 nation states of the African continent is taking place in October, in Nigeria.

2023.04.17(THU) ~ 23(WED)

@ Eagle Square, Abuja, Nigeria

Lecture

Listen to the speakers from various countries talk about the hitory and prospects of African storytelling.

Connect

Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with experts for each topic.

Workshop
