AFRICAN FICTION FESTIVAL 2023

The Fiction Festival brings together the community of writers, story-tellers, actors and other artists who comprise the art world together once every year. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of African literature.
